Refund Policy

All payments on the ei-apps platform are processed by Paddle.com, acting as Merchant of Record for all orders.

Refund requests are reviewed in accordance with Paddle policies and applicable consumer protection laws. To initiate a refund request, contact Paddle customer support directly at: https://paddle.com

EU/EEA consumers are entitled to rights under the Consumer Rights Directive (2011/83/EU), including the right of withdrawal within 14 days, subject to possible limitations for digital services that have been actively used.

Contact: info@ei-apps.com
