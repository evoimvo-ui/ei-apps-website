Work Records

How it works

Everything you need to manage your work, clients and team — in one simple app.

Open app View pricing
New Entry
Log payments and debts in seconds

Here you log a payment or debt for a client. Optionally select a client from your existing list or create a new one. Click + in the Service field to add a custom service and set its approximate duration. Choose the entry type — Paid or Debt — enter the amount and save. Entries sync to the cloud automatically.

  • Select or create a client from your list
  • Add custom services with duration
  • Log paid services or outstanding debts
  • Automatic cloud sync after every save
Appointments
Schedule and manage client appointments

Schedule appointments for your clients. Select a date on the calendar, click 'Book appointment', enter the client's name, phone number, time and service. Choose the notification platform — WhatsApp or Viber. The app will remind you 24 hours and 1 hour before the appointment, with the option to send the reminder directly to your client.

  • Calendar view with easy date navigation
  • WhatsApp or Viber client notifications
  • Reminders at 24h and 1h before the appointment
  • Send reminders directly from the app
Overview
Full picture of your earnings and debts

A full view of all entries with filters by day, week, month and year. For each entry you see: date and time, client name, service, amount and payment status. Debts are clearly marked — click 'Collect' to record a debt payment. Total earnings and outstanding debts are shown at the top. The shop owner sees entries from all workers, while each worker sees only their own.

  • Filter by day, week, month or year
  • Search by client name or service
  • Track and collect outstanding debts
  • Owners see all workers, workers see their own
Reports
Earnings at a glance, export in one tap

Earnings statistics for any period with a visual chart. Export all data as a CSV file or print a report directly from the app.

  • Visual earnings chart by day or month
  • Today's earnings always visible
  • Export to CSV for accounting
  • Print-ready report view
Clients
Your client list, always at hand

Keep a list of your regular clients with their contact details. When logging a new entry or booking an appointment, simply select the client from the list instead of typing their name each time. Create a new client directly from the entry form.

  • Save client name and phone number
  • Quick select when logging entries
  • Quick select when booking appointments
  • Create new clients on the fly
Shop & Team
Invite your team, manage everything together

Manage your shop — add the services you offer and invite workers by email. Enter the worker's email in the invitation; when they register with that email, they automatically join your shop. Only the owner pays the subscription — workers use the app free of charge within the owner's plan. The owner sees entries, appointments and reports for all workers, while each worker sees only their own data.

  • Invite workers by email — no manual setup
  • Workers join automatically on registration
  • Only the owner pays — workers are free
  • Full team overview for the owner
