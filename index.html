FAQ
Have a question?

Answers to the most common queries from our users. For anything else, feel free to contact us.

Is a credit card required for the trial? +
No. The trial period is entirely free and does not require any payment details. After 30 days you can choose a subscription plan or simply stop using the app — no obligations.
Can I cancel my subscription at any time? +
Yes. You can cancel at any time with no early termination fee. Access to the app remains active until the end of the already paid billing period.
What devices does the app work on? +
The app is web-based and works in all modern browsers — Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge. It is optimised for desktop, tablet, and mobile. Native Android and iOS apps are in development.
Is my data secure? +
Yes. All data is transmitted and stored using industry-standard security protocols. Your business information is never shared with third parties.
